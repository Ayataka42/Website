Photography

Capturing moments through my lens

My journey began with the school yearbook, where I discovered the power of capturing life's moments in a single frame. I specialize in street photography, documenting the beauty in everyday scenes, while also covering events for sports and organizations like my school's robotics club.

Open to exploring new styles, particularly portrait photography. If my work interests you, feel free to reach out.
